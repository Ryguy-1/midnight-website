Privacy Policy

Welcome to Midnight! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Key Features

  • Minute Message Lifespan: Your messages will self-destruct in mere minutes. It's like Snapchat, but for people who think even that's too long.
  • Star System: Send messages, earn stars, gain clout.

Information Collection and Use

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Here's how we handle your information:

  • We do not store your messages. Once they are sent and viewed, they are permanently deleted from our servers.
  • We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function, such as user ID and message metadata (timestamps, sender/receiver IDs).
  • We use this information solely to provide the service and improve the app's functionality.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Although we do not store your messages, we implement industry-standard measures to protect the minimal data we do collect from unauthorized access and breaches.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties. Your data remains with us and is used only for providing the services of the Midnight app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes through the app.

Thank you for choosing Midnight! Your secrets are safe with us.
