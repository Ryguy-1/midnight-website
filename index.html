Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Midnight, the app that lets you send self-destructing messages. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Midnight is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and use only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Your email address (for account creation and login)
  • Message content (temporarily stored for delivery)
  • Message timeout settings (to determine when to delete messages)

We do not collect or store phone numbers or any other personal information beyond what's listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of providing the Midnight messaging service:

  • Your email is used for account authentication and to send important account-related notifications
  • Message content is temporarily stored to ensure delivery to the recipient
  • Timeout settings are used to implement the message self-destruct feature

4. Message Deletion

Messages sent through Midnight are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers after the timeout period specified by the sender. Once a message is deleted, it cannot be recovered by anyone, including us.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information and ensure the confidentiality of your messages while they are stored on our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how our app is used. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the bottom of this policy.
